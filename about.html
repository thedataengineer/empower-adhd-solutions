About Me

Braden Young

Certified ADHD Life Coach · Wenatchee, Washington
Founder, Empower ADHD Solutions

I'm Braden, a certified ADHD life coach, and someone who got his own ADHD diagnosis at 36. Like a lot of late-diagnosed adults, that diagnosis rewrote the story of everything that came before it. It is also why I do this work.

Outside of 1-on-1 coaching, I founded and run the Wenatchee ADHD Support Group, facilitate the Eastern Washington chapter of the Men's ADHD Support Group, and write regularly on the blog. I share my life with my wife, an adult stepdaughter, and a young son who arrived in the middle of the pandemic, so yes, I know exactly what parenting with ADHD feels like from the inside.

I blend art with analysis. My interests run across creativity, science, and technology, and my coaching reflects that mix: part practical systems-building, part reframing what your brain is actually good at.

Why I do this

My goal is bigger than my own practice: educate people about ADHD, and fight the stigma and misinformation that still surround it. Neurodivergent perspectives are not defects to be tolerated; they are a source of real contribution and innovation. The more people understand that, the better life gets for every neurodivergent person, diagnosed or not.
