context switching

Slack ate your flow state

Every interruption costs an ADHD brain more than it costs your coworkers. Ten "quick questions" a day and your deep work window never opens.

estimation

Time blindness meets sprint planning

You estimate in good faith. Your brain does not track time in a way that makes those estimates mean anything. Every retro feels like a personal indictment.

boring tickets

The two-day task on week three

Not hard, not interesting, not started. Interest-based attention means the routine migration sits there while you rebuild the logging pipeline nobody asked for.

on-call

Great in the incident, wrecked after

You are the person you want in a sev-1. Adrenaline focuses the ADHD brain. The crash afterward, and the paperwork, is where it falls apart.

perf reviews

Brilliant, but inconsistent

The feedback never changes. High peaks, mystery valleys, and a promotion doc that undersells you because you forgot half of what you shipped.

remote work

WFH removed the guardrails

No commute, no body doubling, no external structure. Remote work gave you freedom and took away every scaffold you did not know you were using.

Your brain is not a bug

The traits that make ADHD hard in a sprint are the same ones that make you valuable in a career. Hyperfocus ships the gnarly refactor. Pattern recognition spots the design flaw in review. Novelty seeking is why you learn new stacks faster than your team. Crisis response makes you calm when production is down.

Coaching does not sand those edges off. It builds the scaffolding so the costs stop eating the benefits: externalized memory, time systems that assume time blindness, task starts that do not depend on willpower, and honest workload negotiation with your manager.

  • Capture systems that survive context switches
  • Sprint and estimation strategies built for interest-based attention
  • Deep work protection that coexists with Slack culture
  • Perf review and promo doc prep that records wins as they happen
  • Burnout prevention for the always-shipping treadmill
