Client Hub

Starting coaching, or already working with me? Everything you need is here.

Step 1

Discovery Call

Free 30 minutes. We talk about where you are, what you want to change, and whether coaching is the right tool. No pressure either way.

Step 2

Intake Form

Before our first full session, the intake form below gives me the context to make session one count, your history, your goals, what has and has not worked.

Step 3

Regular Sessions

Weekly or monthly, we build systems, test them against real life, and adjust. Between sessions you have experiments to run, not homework to dread.

New Client Intake Form

Fill this out before your first session. It goes straight to Braden and stays confidential.

Session Prep

Five minutes before each session, jot down:

  • One win since last time, however small
  • One thing that fell apart, and when you noticed
  • The experiment we agreed on: did it run? What happened?
  • What you want out of the next hour

Need to reschedule or have a question between sessions? Contact page or email Braden@EmpowerADHDSolutions.com.
