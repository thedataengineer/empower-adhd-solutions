ADHD Coaching

Personalized, strength-based coaching for adults with ADHD, moving from struggling through each week to building a life that fits how your brain works.

What is ADHD coaching?

ADHD coaching is specialized life coaching. Together we develop concrete, tailored strategies for the things ADHD makes hard, starting tasks, managing time, following through, staying organized, and we build toward the goals that matter to you. It is practical, forward-looking work: less about why you are the way you are, more about what works for the brain you have.

What an ADHD coach is not

  • Not a therapist. Coaching focuses on present and future goals, not treating psychological conditions.
  • Not a doctor. I do not diagnose ADHD or prescribe or manage medication.
  • Not a tutor. The work is life systems, not academic subjects.
  • Not a mentor handing you a template. Your strategies get built around your brain, your life, and your goals.

Why work with an ADHD coach?

  • Strategies designed around how your brain actually works
  • Real accountability between sessions
  • Goal-setting that survives contact with an ADHD week
  • Time management and planning systems you will actually use
  • Confidence built on evidence, not affirmations
