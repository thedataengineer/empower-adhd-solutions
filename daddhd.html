The DadDHD Podcast

Fatherhood with ADHD and autism, talked about out loud.

There is a huge gap in the conversation around neurodivergent parenting, and dads sit squarely in it. DadDHD is about the everyday realities nobody covers in the clinical literature: getting through a bedtime routine when time blindness is winning, staying regulated when your kid is melting down, keeping a partnership close when executive dysfunction keeps stealing the follow-through.

Episodes bring on expert guests across ADHD, autism, parenting, and mental health — and real parents telling the truth about their experience, from NICU stays to intimacy. We are building the show in real time, focused on what actually helps people rather than a rigid format.

It is aimed at dads, but any parent navigating neurodivergence is welcome here. The project is a collaboration with multiple ADHD and parenting support organizations.
